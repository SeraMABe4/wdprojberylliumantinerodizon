(United States)

Hamburger Recipe
picture
description A beef patty served in a bun, often with toppings like lettuce, cheese, and condiments.
materials 1. a frying pan
2. Tongs or Spatula
3. Medium Bowl
4. Whisk
5. Fork or Hands
6. Instant-Read Thermometer
7. Measuring Spoon
ingredients 1. one large egg
2. one-half teaspoon salt
3. one-half teaspoon ground black pepper
4. one pound ground beef
5. one-half cup fine dry bread crumbs
6. cooking oil
procedure 1. Preheat a pan for high heat and put cooking oil.
2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.
4. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.
Place patties on the preheated pan. Cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

Biscuits and Gravy Recipe
picture
description Soft, fluffy biscuits topped with a creamy sausage gravy.
materials 1. saucepan
2. papertowel
3. wooden spoon
4. plate
ingredients 1. Pork sausage/bacon
2. Flour
3. Butter
4. Milk
5. Thyme
6. Rosemary
7. Crushed red pepper
8.Store-bought biscuits
procedure 1. Place down biscuits on the plate
2. Put the sausage or bacon in a large saucepan; use a paper towel to blot out most of the grease from the pan

4. Cook on medium-low, stir it slowly for several minutes until thickened

Meal 3

(United Kingdom)

Mac and Cheese Recipe
picture
description Pasta in a rich, creamy cheese sauce.
materials 1. Pot
2. Strainer
3. Large bowl
4. Whisk
5. Spoon
ingredients 1. 2 cups elbow macaroni
2. 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
3. 1 1/2 cups milk
4. 2 tbsp butter
5. 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
6. Salt and pepper
procedure 1. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water for 8-10 minutes, then drain
2. In a pot, melt butter over medium heat
3. Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute
4. Slowly whisk in milk, cooking until the sauce thickens (about 3-5 minutes)
5. Stir in cheese, salt, and pepper until melted and smooth
6. Add cooked macaroni and mix until well coated

European Cuisine
Meal 1

(England)

Fish and Chips Recipe
picture
description Fried fish fillets served with crispy fried potato fries.
materials 1. Frying pan/deep fryer
2. Bowl
3. Tongs
4. Paper towels
5. Strainer
ingredients 1. 4 white fish fillets
2. 1 cup all-purpose flour
3. 1 cup cold sparkling water/beer
4. 1 tsp baking powder
5. Salt and pepper
6. 4 large potatoes
7. Oil for frying
procedure 1. Peel and cut potatoes into fries
2. Soak them in water for 30 minutes, then pat dry
3. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper
4. Gradually whisk in sparkling water/beer until smooth
5. Heat oil in a pan or fryer to 350°F (175°C)
6. Fry chips in batches for 4-5 minutes until golden
7. Remove and drain on paper towels
8. Dip fish fillets in batter and fry in hot oil for 5-7 minutes until golden and crispy
9. Drain on paper towels
10. Serve the fish with chips and optional tartar sauce

Meal 2

(Italy)

Arancini Recipe
picture
description Fried rice balls filled with cheese, meat, or vegetables.
materials 1. Pot
2. Bowl
3. Spoon
4. Deep fryer or large pan
5. Paper towels
ingredients 1. 2 cups cooked risotto
2. 1/2 cup cubed mozzarella
3. 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
4. 1 beaten egg
5. 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
6. 1 cup breadcrumbs
7. Oil for frying
8. Salt and pepper
procedure 1. Mix risotto with Parmesan, salt, and pepper
2. Form into balls, adding a cube of mozzarella in the center of each
3. Dredge each ball in flour, dip in beaten egg, and coat with breadcrumbs
4. Heat oil in a pan or fryer to 350°F (175°C)
5. Fry arancini in batches for 3-4 minutes until golden and crispy
6. Drain on paper towels
7. Serve hot, with marinara sauce

(Germany)

Currywurst Recipe
picture
description German sausage topped with curry-flavored ketchup and served with fries.
materials 1. Grill/frying pan
2. Knife
3. Saucepan
4. Spoon
ingredients 1. 4 German sausages (bratwurst or similar)
2. 1 cup ketchup
3. 2 tbsp curry powder
4. 1 tbsp paprika
5. 1 tbsp sugar
6. 1 tbsp vinegar
7. Salt
8. Oil
procedure 1. Grill or fry the sausages until fully cooked and browned on all sides (about 8-10 minutes)
2. Slice them into bite-sized pieces
3. In a saucepan, mix ketchup, curry powder, paprika, sugar, vinegar, and salt
4. Heat over low-medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
5. Drizzle curry sauce over the sliced sausages

Asian Cuisine
Meal 1

(Thailand)

Pad Thai Recipe
picture
description Stir-fried rice noodles with eggs, tofu or shrimp, peanuts, and a tangy sauce.
materials 1. wok/large frying pan
2. bowl
3. Spoon/spatula
4. knife
ingredients 1. 8 oz rice noodles
2. 2 tbsp vegetable oil
3. 1 beaten egg
4. 1/2 cup tofu or shrimp
5. 1/4 cup peanuts
6. 1/4 cup green chopped onions
7. 2 tbsp fish sauce
8. 1 tbsp soy sauce
9. 1 tbsp tamarind paste
10. 1 tbsp sugar
11. 1 wedged-cut lime
12. 1/4 tsp chili flakes
procedure 1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions
2. Drain and set aside
3. In a wok, heat 1 tbsp oil over medium heat
4. Cook tofu or shrimp until done, then remove and set aside
5. In the same wok, add the remaining oil
6. Scramble the beaten egg, then add cooked noodles, fish sauce, soy sauce, tamarind paste, and sugar
7. Stir-fry for 2 to 3 minutes
8. Add tofu or shrimp, green onions, and chili flakes
9. Stir everything together
10. Top with crushed peanuts and lime wedges

Meal 2

(India)

Biryani Recipe
picture
description Spiced rice dish often cooked with chicken, lamb, or vegetables.
materials 1. Large pot or pressure cooker
2. Bowl
3. Spoon
4. Strainer
5. Frying pan (optional for onions)
ingredients 1. 2 cups basmati rice that's soaked for 30 minutes
2. 1 lb chicken/lamb/vegetables
3. 1 sliced onion
4. 2 chopped tomatoes
5. 1/4 cup plain yogurt
6. 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
7. 2 tbsp biryani masala or curry powder
8. 1 tsp cumin seeds
9. 1 cinnamon stick
10. 4 to 5 cloves
11. 2 to 3 cardamom pods
12. 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
13. 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
14. 1/4 cup fried onions (optional)
15. 4 cups water
16. 2 tbsp ghee or oil
17. Salt
procedure 1. In a pot, boil water with a pinch of salt and cumin seeds.
2. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done
3. Drain and set aside
4. Heat ghee or oil in a pan
5. Saute onions until golden
6. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
7. Cook until tomatoes soften
8. Add chicken and yogurt, cook for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is cooked through
9. Layer half of the partially cooked rice, then the cooked chicken mixture, remaining rice, cilantro, mint, and fried onions
10. Cover tightly, and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or use a pressure cooker without the weight for 10 minutes
11. Gently fluff the biryani and serve hot with raita or salad

Meal 3

(Philippines)

Chicken Adobo Recipe
picture
description Filipino dish with chicken braised in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices.
materials 1. Pot/Dutch oven
2. Spoon
3. Knife
4. Bowl
ingredients 1. 1.5 lbs chicken (cut into pieces)
2. 1/4 cup soy sauce
3. 1/4 cup vinegar
4. 6 garlic cloves (crushed)
5. 2 bay leaves
6. 1 tbsp black peppercorns
7. 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
8. 1 cup water
9. 2 tbsp oil (for frying)
10. Salt
procedure 1. In a bowl, combine chicken, soy cauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, and sugar.
2. Marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight)
3. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat.
4. Add the chicken along with the marinade.
5. Cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
6. Add water, bring to a simmer, and cook for 20-30 minutes until the chicken is tender and the sauce thickens.
7. Adjust seasoning with salt, then serve hot with rice.
