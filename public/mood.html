MIX AND MOOD




You've probably already seen the long list of meals you can choose from! Its probably got you feeling super overwhelmed, maybe even a bit tired? For some people, they cure this dillema with food! Have you ever heard of comfort food? It's when you stuff yourself with ice cream until you feel better, or stuff yourself with any kind of food actually. However, it's not only limited to feeling sad, you can eat food when you're angry, tired, or even happy. How about you, what's your mood?

Pick from the dropdown menu below on what you're feeling, and we'll give you a meal that you'll eat till you're feeling even happier! Once you've seen the ingredients, try to get it in the correct order to get the supposed meal. If you're feeling stuck, visit the Recipes or Follow Along pages.
