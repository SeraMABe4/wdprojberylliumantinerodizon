Cuisine Biography

American Cuisine
American cuisine usually consists of bold and hearty flavors. They're big fans of spices such as cumin, paprika, etc. They like to make sure their dishes aren't bland to the palate. They like to incorporate many types of cooking in their cuisine, being fans of things like garlic, soy sauce and MSG. Of course, we can't forget their classic dishes, like hamburgers, hotdogs, and numerous kinds of fried foods.
European Cuisine
European's seem to be fans of their meat and flour, as many European dishes are made of them. Italy's pizza, made of flour. Sweden's meatballs, made with meat. There is also usually a salad along with your meal. They love things like steak and mashed potatoes, favorites in the continent. You may think that European food is a bit bland, but really, it isn't. You've just gotta get used to it.
Asian Cuisine
Asian cuisine can be described as savory, and full of that umami flavor. They like to use an array of flavors, making their food, sweet, sour, salty, spicy and even bitter. They also love using fresh ingredients, enhancing the flavors of each dish. With these fresh ingredients come many health benefits. The umami flavor of their dishes really stand out when eating broth or kinds of soup. Asian cuisine can be a lot of things.
